# Vrinda Store Sales Performance Dashboard (2022) - Project Plan

This document outlines the scope, structure, and objectives for the **Vrinda Store Sales Performance Dashboard (2022)** project, based on the provided data analysis plan.

---

## 1. Project Overview

1. **Project Title:** Vrinda Store Sales Performance Dashboard (2022)
2. **Objective:** Analyze sales performance to identify key factors driving revenue, customer segments contributing most to profit, and opportunities to improve marketing and operations.
3. **Dataset Source:** Vrinda Store Data Analysis.xlsx
   *(Expected fields: order date, customer name, gender, state, product category, sales, quantity, profit, payment mode, etc.)*

---

## 2. Tableau Dashboard Plan

### 2.1 Dashboard 1: Sales Overview

1. **Purpose:** Provide a high-level snapshot of business performance.
2. **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

   1. Total Sales
   2. Total Profit
   3. Number of Orders
   4. Average Order Value
3. **Visuals:**

   1. Sales Trend (Monthly Line Chart)
   2. Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
   3. Profit by State (Map)
   4. Sales by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)
4. **Interactivity:**

   1. Filters: Year, Month, Category, State
   2. Dynamic tooltips with order details

---

### 2.2 Dashboard 2: Customer Insights

1. **Purpose:** Understand who the best customers are and their buying patterns.
2. **Visuals:**

   1. Sales by Gender (Pie or Bar Chart)
   2. Age Group vs Total Sales (Stacked Bar Chart)
   3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue (Horizontal Bar Chart)
   4. State-wise Customer Distribution (Map)
3. **Interactivity:**

   1. Click on gender to filter all other charts.
   2. Hover over the map to view top-performing states.

---

### 2.3 Dashboard 3: Product Performance & Profitability

1. **Purpose:** Identify products and categories driving the most profit and highlight underperformers.
2. **Visuals:**

   1. Category vs Profit (Bar Chart)
   2. Sub-Category vs Sales & Profit (Dual-axis Chart)
   3. Discount vs Profit Margin (Scatter Plot)
   4. Top 10 Products by Profit (List/Table)
3. **Interactivity:**

   1. Category dropdown to filter subcategory visuals.
   2. Dynamic highlight for profitable products.

---

## 3. Business Questions to Answer

### 3.1 Sales & Profit Trends

1. Which months generated the highest and lowest revenue?
2. Which payment method was most preferred by customers?

### 3.2 Customer Insights

1. Do male or female customers contribute more to sales?
2. Which age group spends the most money?
3. Which states have the most loyal or high-spending customers?

### 3.3 Product Performance

1. Which product category is most profitable?
2. Which products need to be discontinued or discounted less?
3. Is there a relationship between discount and profit margin?

### 3.4 Business Decisions

1. Based on data, which customer segment should be targeted more?
2. Which products or regions should get higher marketing budgets?
3. How can Vrinda Store increase profit without losing customers?
